About

Research lead specialising in healthcare, pharma, and medtech. I lead complex, months-long design & market research programmes in high-ambiguity environments — where clinical practice is evolving, terminology and clinical workflows vary across markets, innovation is shaped by a cascade of decisions that are ever-changing.

My work spans qualitative and quantitative research; from observations worldwide in clinical environments, to designing and launching global surveys across multiple geographies, to leading a small research & UI execution team, to moderating and orchestrating multilingual interview programmes, synthesising diverse data streams, and translating complexity into clear strategic direction.

I work to bring the unfiltered reality of the user's experience and clinical reality to the forefront of every strategic decision, bridging the gap between the clinical floor and the development team.

I currently lead major client programmes for the world's leading pharma/medtech organisation, partnering closely with cross-functional stakeholders to deliver research that is rigorous, actionable, and immediately useful. I don't just execute and present, I work collaboratively to shape the right questions, adapt the approach as evidence emerges, and ensure insights can be used in practice for the team I work with — not shelved. I also use AI in a controlled, deliberate way to accelerate analysis and scale research operations while maintaining quality, judgement, and methodological integrity.
