Meet Mitch — research lead, ethnographer, innovation & strategist.

Research lead specialising in healthcare, pharma, and medtech. I currently lead major client programmes for the world's leading pharma/medtech organisation, partnering closely with cross-functional stakeholders to deliver research that is rigorous, actionable, and immediately useful.

My work spans from observations worldwide in clinical environments, to designing and launching global surveys across multiple geographies, to leading multilingual interview programmes, synthesising diverse data streams, and translating complexity into clear strategic direction.

I don't just execute and present — I work collaboratively to shape the right questions, adapt the approach as evidence emerges, and ensure insights reach the right people at the right moment.

I'm an audiobook listener, escape room enthusiast (I've even designed a few, which were smashing if I might add), and a mother of identical twins who are the least alike you'll ever meet.
